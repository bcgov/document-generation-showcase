6. Have proper hand-washing facilities: At work-site and accommodation

Helping workers to engage in hand hygiene prevents or reduces the spread of COVID-19 and other illnesses. Industrial Camp operators should ensure easy access to hand hygiene facilities either through hand hygiene stations or the provisions of hand sanitizer at the work site and at the accommodation site.

 

[{d.attestation.handWashingStations:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}] I have an adequate number of hand washing stations (either permanent or portable) available to workers

[{d.attestation.handWashingSoapWater:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}] There is an appropriate supply of soap and water

[{d.attestation.handWashingWaterless:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}] I have supplemented with waterless hand sanitizers with a min of 60% alcohol where appropriate

[{d.attestation.handWashingPaperTowels:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}] I have provided disposable paper towels.

[{d.attestation.handWashingSignage:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}] I have put up signs to promote regular hand washing.

 

7. Physical distancing practices

Keeping a 2-metre distance between people is one of the most important ways to break the chain of transmission of COVID-19.  Industrial Camp operators can take practical steps to ensure physical distancing is maintained while workers are transported to or from the work site, while working indoors or outdoors, and during break-times.

 

Physical barriers such as the use of plexi-glass, face shields, masks, and other techniques can be used where physical distancing is not possible. 

 

[{d.attestation.distancingMaintained:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}] I have taken steps to ensure physical distancing can be maintained during work and after work.

[{d.attestation.distancingFaceShields:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}] I have physical barriers like face shields or masks for situations where physical distancing is not possible.

8. Have a cleaning and disinfecting schedule

All common areas and surfaces should be cleaned at the start and end of each day. Examples of common areas and surfaces include washrooms, common tables, desks, light switches, and door handles. Regular household cleaners are effective against COVID-19, following the instructions on the label.

 

[{d.attestation.disinfectingSchedule:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}] I have a schedule to ensure common and high touch areas are cleaned or disinfected at the start and end of each day.

3. After workers arrive at the Industrial Camp

Once your workers arrive at the location, you are expected to continue your work enacting an infection prevention control plan and minimize the risk of transmission of COVID-19 through the following key activities.

1. Implement COVID-19 Employee Education

Industrial Camp operators need to make workers aware of the risks of COVID-19 and be prepared if workers have questions about COVID-19.

 

[{d.attestation.educationSignage:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}] I have signage in place in the appropriate language on how employees can protect themselves from COVID-19

[{d.attestation.educationContactPersonnel:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}]I have someone identified that workers can go to if they have question on COVID-19

2. Train workers on COVID-19 infection control

Industrial Camp operators must provide workers with training in a language that is accessible to them about the risk of COVID-19, safe work practices, and how to report symptoms.

[{d.attestation.mealsDishwashing:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}] Used dishware will be washed immediately.

4. If workers become ill at the Industrial Camp

1. Plan to manage individuals with suspected COVID-19 Infection

Industrial Camp operators must have a plan and protocol to deal with workers demonstrating symptoms of COVID-19, including immediate self-isolation of the worker and notifying the local health authority.

If two or more workers become sick, you must notify the local Medical Health Officer of the outbreak.

 

[{d.attestation.infectionSeparation:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}] I am prepared to promptly separate the individual from others in their own accommodation.

[{d.attestation.infectionSymptoms:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}] I am prepared to provide individuals exhibiting symptoms of COVID-19 with a surgical/procedural mask or tissues to cover their mouth and nose.

[{d.attestation.infectionHeathLinkBC:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}] I am prepared to direct the person to call HealthLinkBC (8-1-1).

[{d.attestation.infectionSanitization:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}] I am prepared to clean and disinfect any rooms that the person has been in while symptomatic.

[{d.attestation.infectionAccommodation:ifEQ(true):show('Yes'):elseShow('___')}] If commercial accommodation is being used to self-isolate, then I will inform management of the situation and necessary requirements.
